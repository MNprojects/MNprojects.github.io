About

I'm Michael. I'm a recent graduate focusing on software development, living and working in London.

For the past 18 months, I've been working full-time in a tech consultant role. As the main contact for 15 clients, they would come to me for any of their technical needs. I gave each of my clients the bespoke service they needed, whether that was looking after their servers, users, email accounts, archiving of data, budgeting and scheduling, or proactive maintenance.

While continuing to work, over the past 3 years, I have been studying BSc (Hons) Computing and IT with the Open University. I graduated this year with a 2:2, focusing on software engineering.

Right now, I'm looking for opportunities to get into software development. I'm particularly interested in back-end systems or game development.

I find that programming is a creative outlet for me. I love building things and programming is another way of doing just that. One of my hobbies is woodworking and as I learn more about computing I'm looking to integrate it into my projects.
